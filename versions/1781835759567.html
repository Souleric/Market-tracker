Opportunist playbook by horizon

Short-term / reactive (days–weeks)
  • Trade the headline spike: oil & gold pop on strikes, fade partially once retaliation is "priced." First search result this cycle: gold gave back most of its spike after the initial attack.
  • USD/JPY & CHF are the fastest risk-on/off tells — yen & franc bid on fear, sold on calm.
  • Hormuz headlines = the single biggest oil catalyst. ~30% of seaborne oil transits it.
  • Keep position size small around binary events; gaps are violent in both directions.
Swing / positioning (weeks–months)
  • Defense is the cleanest structural trade: Trump's $1.5T 2027 budget plan; A&D ETF +10.5% in a week. LMT, RTX, NOC, plus EU names.
  • Energy majors (XOM, CVX, Shell) carry the oil premium with dividends if Brent stays >$90.
  • A sustained Brent spike is stagflationary — pressures the Fed, supports gold, weighs on rate-sensitive equities.
  • Asia tail risk argues for some JPY / gold / USD hedge if you hold Taiwan/HK/China exposure (TSM, Nikkei, Hang Seng).
